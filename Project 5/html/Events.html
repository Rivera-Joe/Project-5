Vaccination Event:

Come visit us on the second Saturday of every month
if your a pet owner who would like to get their cat or dog vaccinated!

we host a bi-weekly event for reduced vaccination cost.

Adoption Event:

Come visit us on the fourth Saturday of every month
if you are looking to adopt a loveable new friend!
Though you are welcome to inquire about adoption at any time,
we host a monthly adoption event.
